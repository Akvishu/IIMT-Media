Library

A well stocked Library is the backbone of any Academic Institution. The library plays a very important role and shares its commitment towards achieving excellence in academia.
Each College of IIMT has its own well stocked Library with a spacious reading room. The Library has total stock of more than 5Lac books and publications and more than 600 National & International Journals in Management, Engineering, Law, Education, Pharmacy, etc.
The Library also contains rich collection of assorted material on General Awareness that can be used by the students. The Institute is also the member of DELNET, INDEST & EDUSET that provides ready access to the readers to the libraries of leading Institutions of the world.
Online database subscription for data related to Industries and business are also available as supportive research materials for faculties and the students.
Special Audio-Video lectures are arranged in Library for students. Students can also login different research oriented professional societies to enhance their learning & research.
