SPORTS

We believe that perfect blend of sports and academics ensure all round development of the students. A healthy mind dwells in a healthy body. Keeping in mind the objective of all round development of the students, various sporting and physical activities are promoted. Several modern indoor and outdoor games facilities have been developed. Students are encouraged to participate in various sports like Badminton, Football, Basket Ball, Volley Ball, Cricket Table Tennis, Carrom & chess etc.
