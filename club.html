Student Club


Students Clubs and organizations allow further exploration and discovery in both academics and non academic areas, enabling growth experiences & learning beyond the classroom settings.

Vedik Club : Value Education for Divine Inspiration and Culture (VEDIC) club is for the adolescent youth. This club is for the welfare for the youth and the main aim of this club is to give the way of real happiness for the wisdom of everyone’s life.This club also helps in the overall personality development of students of the college.
