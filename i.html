Industrial Visits

We at IIMT understand the importance of practical exposure along with theoretical knowledge. The CRC at IIMT provides opportunities to the students to learn and upgrade their “Theory based Technical Knowledge” to practical expertise through well planned Industrial Visits.

This Industry-Institute Conglomerate helps bright minds to learn “Practical concepts” through interaction, learning working methods & understanding employment practices.
