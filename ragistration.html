(#). Admission Procedure for AKTU Courses:-

{1.}Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) conducts an entrance examination for 85% seats.The candidates are allotted branches on the basis of their merit through counseling conducted by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU).

{2.}For 15% seats candidates can apply for direct admission to the IIMT. Admission will be based on the percentage of marks obtained in qualifying exam and /or respective entrance exam as per the policies of the University.

{3.}Candidates seeking admission to the first semester of the six-semester MCA course are required to qualify in the Common Entrance Examination (UPSEE) for admission to Master of Computer Application.

{4.}The eligibility criteria for direct admission on seats remaining vacant in first year after entrance examination counseling shall be such as may be notified from time to time by AKTU.

(#). Admission Procedure for UPBTE Course:-

(1). Admission to this course is done by combined BTE Entrance Examination conducted by UP state Government followed by counseling.

(2). Polytechnic Courses are the basic engineering courses. A candidate can take admission through Entrance Examination UPJEE (Polytechnic) or direct against laps seats in first year after passing of HSC/ 10th with minimum 35% marks or can take admission direct in Second year (Lateral entry) after SSC/ 12th with Science with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with one of the Mathematics/ Biology (Botany and Zoology) / Vocational/ Technical Subjects/ ITI with appropriate Trade.
