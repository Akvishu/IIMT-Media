IIMT GROUP OF COLLEGES, MEERUT

IIMT Engineering College - A Glimpse
THE IIMT Engineering College, Meerut (College Code 127) was established in 2001 at Ganga Nagar, Meerut with an objective to create technically competent engineers who can play a major role in development of nation. The College offers B. Tech in IT, CS, EC, EN, ME and Civil branches with an a total intake of 990 students and M. Tech in ME, ECE, CSE & IT.
