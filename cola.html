Collaborations

At IIMT, the CRC strives to promote and nurture close interaction with the industrial sector and play a positive role in the growth of its students across various branches. The activities of the CRC are focused at effectively utilizing the manpower and other resources available within the Institute, with the guidance and assistance of collaborated companies, for strengthening the Industry-Institute needs of the companies across the region by improving the quality of professional training of IIMT students in general.
